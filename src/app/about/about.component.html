Welcome to Highland Oaks! We are a subdivision consisting of 259 homes in Gwinnett County in Lawrenceville, Georgia. Our amenities include a beautiful swimming pool, 2 tennis courts, and a pavilion with picnic tables and fireplace.

Our neighborhood is an active Homeowners Association that abides by covenants to ensure well-maintained properties. Our HOA is managed by Association Management Advisory Group.
